Q&A


Do you provide tours outside of EU?

We arrange guided tours in EU and EEA countries, and Switzerland. We do not provide our services in other European countries e.g. Turkey, Montenegro, Serbia etc. You can find all destinations in our tour catalog.

Do I need visa to book a tour?

You do not need visa to book a tour, however, you might need visa to enter the destination country. EU-citizens can travel visa free inside the EU. Non-EU resident might need visa and/or other travel document. Please, contact us to review your situation.

Can I modify the tour?

It is possible to make changes in the tour, however it might cause additional expenses.

How do you treat customers with special diets?

We understand that our customers may have food restrictions or allergies. Most of our partner restaurants provide vegetarian/vegan options, as well as gluten-free and non-dairy dishes. We try to make sure that everyone receives a full experience of our tours.

What is a minimum number of people within the group?

For our tours we require at least 4 people, excluding our seasonal Valentine February tour (min. 2 people).
